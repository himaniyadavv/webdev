Who Are We?

We are an education company solving a problem we all face -
choosing and navigating the right career path.

Mentorbox brings to you the best minds in the industry to collaborate
and get mentored. We have professionals ranging from top-notch
companies such to individuals who have made it big as entrepreneurs.

We are connecting mentors and mentees under one umbrella and
aiming to build the largest mentorship community.

Our focus areas include career mentorship, soft skill development and
strategic coaching to make our mentees career ready.

Our mentorship sessions have already helped hundreds of students in
choosing the best career option and getting insights into their aspired career fields.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create the largest community that is able to envision, build and market

original and innovative products through our structured approach to mentorship, strategic

coaching and soft-skills development,

Our Founder

Miss Anapurna Monga is an Alumnus from the prestigious Motilal Nehru National
Institute of Technology(MNNIT) and has experience working as a Software Engineer on Logistics, Healthcare and Payments Domain.

She has also previously co-founded a startup named ‘Jivasutra’ which was basically an Ayurvedic services aggregator and focused on providing holistic healing to end-users. She was recently working with India’s fastest growing payment domain start-up 'PineLabs' where she spearheaded the launching of a new product-EPOS(Electronic-Point-of-Sale) for contactless payments for small merchants amidst the COVID crisis.

​

Anapurna has now again ventured into entrepreneurship with her own Ed-Tech startup, Mentorbox which focuses on providing accessible mentorship to students in schools/colleges as well as young professionals. Her vision is to mentor young talent through her mentorship programs and make the country self-sustainable and inclined towards creating in-house products.

​

She recently started speaking up about women empowerment through her posts with the title 'Women-with-a-Voice'. She is also a motivational speaker and has spoken for many International startups such as Alariss as a distinguished podcast speaker on gender disparity and woman empowerment.
