Plans & Services

Personal Mentorship Sessions

One-on-One video call with an
expert mentor from relevant
industry.

Mentorbox will match you with an
expert mentor based on your needs
and setup an hour session tailor-
made to your needs.

You can also develop a personal
connect with the mentor to stay
connected for follow-up
conversations through our in-built
chat platform.

​ We provide mentorship for shaping
your career goal, preparation for
interviews and competitive exams
such as GMAT, GATE, IES and IAS.

Personal Soft-Skills Sessions

One-on-One video call with a soft
skills trainer with relevant
industry exposure.

Mentorbox believes that personality
development is equally important
for the success of a professional.

​ Our soft skill sessions include
honing presentation skills,
leadership development, insights
into being an entrepreneur, creating
an impact in your first job etc.

​​ We bring to you hour based one-on-
one sessions to hone your skills so
that you are way ahead of your
peers.

Group Mentorship Sessions

Group video calls with
experienced professionals from
relevant industry.

Mentorbox believes that
professionals can realize their true
potentials through mentoring with
industry leaders.

Mentorbox will organize hour
based group sessions with industry
leaders with variant experience
working in top-notch companies.

You can choose to attend from a
number of sessions based on your
needs or reach out to us for tailor-
made sessions for your group with
minimum size of 10 members.
