Our Vision:

Our vision is to create the largest mentorship community that is able to envision and build original and innovative products.

How are we planning to do this?

We plan to give mentorship/coaching and other upskilling sessions to students/budding professionals to create an ecosystem of innovation and brand building. We also plan to stop the brain drain happening in India which is primarily due to lack of exposure in the country which students tend to seek outside India and help retain talent which can build such products through our worldwide network of mentors.
